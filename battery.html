With our OEM diagnostic tools we can assess the state-of-health of every module and match them to the right application — whether that’s back into service in a Volta Zero or into a second-life energy storage system.

Fleet Replacement Supply Genuine OEM modules for operators running Volta Zero trucks. Full vehicle diagnostic support included.

Diagnostic capability

OEM-level battery diagnostics.

Our MIRA workshop is equipped with the original Volta OEM diagnostic tooling. We assess modules to the same standard as the factory — not generic EV test equipment.

Assessment & Qualification

  • State of health (SoH) assessment
  • Cell-level voltage and capacity analysis
  • Module qualification against OEM thresholds
  • Capacity testing — usable vs gross
  • Internal resistance and impedance measurement
  • Charge / discharge cycle profiling

Systems & Data

  • BMS data interrogation and fault history review
  • CAN bus diagnostic data capture
  • Thermal performance and cooling system review
  • Module balance and cell group analysis
  • Full written test report with results
  • OEM tooling — not generic EV scan tools
