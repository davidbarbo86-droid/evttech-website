Designed from zero

A complete rethink of the truck.

By removing the combustion engine, Volta were free to rethink everything from first principles — safety, driver wellbeing and urban compatibility.

👁️ Panoramic Visibility

Central driving position and 220° panoramic windscreen gives class-leading visibility of pedestrians, cyclists and vulnerable road users.

🪑 Driver-Centric Cabin

Bright, spacious, car-like interior designed around driver comfort and welfare. Dramatically reduced NVH versus diesel trucks.

🌿 Zero Tailpipe Emissions

100% electric powertrain. Compliant with all UK Clean Air Zones and ULEZ restrictions. Built for cities committed to zero-emission urban freight.

🏙️ Built for Urban Distribution

Every dimension, turning circle and access feature was designed around last-mile city-centre delivery — not adapted from a motorway truck.
