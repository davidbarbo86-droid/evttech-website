From the team that engineered the Volta Zero.

EVT Tech Ltd is founded by former Volta Trucks engineering leads who took the Volta Zero from blank sheet to production — owning the ground-up design, development, validation and certification of a 16-tonne electric commercial vehicle since 2020. That programme is our credential: full-vehicle delivery across mechanical, electrical, software, test & development and diagnostics, to OEM standards.

Following our acquisition of the UK Volta Trucks assets — including workshop and test equipment — we operate from our own development workshop at Horiba MIRA, with direct access to Europe’s most comprehensive automotive test facility. We continue to support the Volta Zero fleet operating across Europe through remote CAN logging and diagnostics.

We engineer, build, test and sign off — the full V-cycle, in one team.

What we do

Beyond testing, our team delivers engineering design and prototyping support across the full vehicle development cycle — from early CAD concept through to physical prototype builds and embedded software development.

3D CAD — Solidworks

Concept studies, system packaging, detailed part design and 2D drawing production with GD&T. Ergonomics and occupant packaging analysis.

CAE Simulation — Ansys

Structural FEA (linear and non-linear), modal analysis and fatigue prediction. Simulation-led design optimisation before physical build.

Prototype Fabrication

In-house metal fabrication including painted prototypes, rapid prototype casting support and 3D-printed components for early-stage design validation.

Vehicle Builds & Modifications

Full prototype and mule vehicle builds and modifications, managed from our own workshop at Horiba MIRA.

Embedded Software Development

Prototype and mule embedded software development using Simulink and Raptor-Dev — from early concept through to integration and sign-off.

Vehicle Systems Packaging

3D packaging studies for powertrain, battery, thermal and chassis systems within vehicle architecture constraints.

From a single measurement campaign to a full development programme — we deliver rigorous, well-documented results with actionable analysis across every vehicle attribute discipline.

Benchmarking

Subjective and objective benchmarking against target competitors with structured scoring and attribute gap analysis.

Energy Consumption — xEV

Full xEV system energy consumption measurement including charging efficiency, regen recovery and drive cycle analysis.

Driveability & Optimisation

Tip-in/tip-out, creep, launch and pedal feel characterisation with calibration optimisation support.

Longitudinal Performance

0–60, overtaking, gradeability and top speed measurement with full instrumented data capture.

Ride — Wheel Rates & Dampers

Corner weight, wheel rate, damper bench characterisation and on-vehicle correlation to target ride performance.

Handling

Double lane change, steady-state cornering, step steer and transient handling assessment to ISO standards.

Steering & Turning Circle

Static and dynamic steering effort, lock-to-lock characterisation and minimum turning circle assessment.

Brake Performance

Stopping distance, brake fade assessment and regenerative braking performance and feel evaluation.

Road Load Data Acquisition

Full RLDA campaigns on customer or representative routes with QA and delivery in customer-specified formats.

RLDA to Accelerated Test

Rainflow counting, damage equivalence and rig programme development from RLDA data sets.

Accelerated Durability

Accelerated durability route development and management, alongside rig-based durability programme support.

HET / CET

Hot and cold environment testing programme management and execution for thermal and HVAC systems.

Ergonomics & Occupant Comfort

Reach and vision analysis, WBV measurement and occupant comfort subjective evaluation.

Wind Tunnel Testing

Aerodynamic drag, lift and cross-wind sensitivity with correlation to road-based coast-down testing.

HMI & UI Assessment

Driver HMI usability, display legibility, control intuitiveness and driver workload assessment.

NVH

In-cabin and exterior NVH measurement, source-path-receiver analysis and pass-by noise assessment.

We have developed complete vehicle control software in-house — from systems architecture through to HIL validation and sign-off — covering the full embedded software development cycle to recognised functional-safety guidelines and engineering best practice.

In-house Control Software

Ground-up development of vehicle control, body control and vehicle safety module software for a production electric commercial vehicle — architecture, implementation and integration.

Functional Safety & Best Practice

Development to recognised functional-safety guidelines and automotive best practice, with requirements traceability and structured, auditable sign-off.

Model Based Systems Engineering

Systems architecture and requirements management using Enterprise Architect, CAPELLA and PREEvision.

MiL / SIL Simulation

Model-in-the-Loop and Software-in-the-Loop validation from early simulation through to pre-hardware sign-off, using Matlab Simulink and New Eagle RCM 112.

HIL Testing

Hardware-in-the-loop testing for VCUs and ECUs using the dSPACE SCALEXIO toolchain (LabBox & AutoBox) in a repeatable, safe test environment.

Network Analysis & Testing

CAN/LIN/Ethernet network analysis and automated testing using CANalyzer, CANoe and Vtest Studio.

Functionality Sign-off

Structured functional verification against requirements — traceable, documented and auditable.

Software Troubleshooting

Root cause investigation using CAN, LIN and Ethernet diagnostic tools.

Fleet Data Monitoring

Telematics-based monitoring, anomaly detection and diagnostic data interpretation for connected vehicle fleets.

Charging & Energy
