Testing services

The full range of vehicle attribute testing.

From a single measurement campaign to a full development programme — we deliver rigorous, well-documented results with actionable analysis across every vehicle attribute discipline.

Benchmarking

Subjective and objective benchmarking against target competitors with structured scoring and attribute gap analysis.

Energy Consumption — xEV

Full xEV system energy consumption measurement including charging efficiency, regen recovery and drive cycle analysis.

Driveability & Optimisation

Tip-in/tip-out, creep, launch and pedal feel characterisation with calibration optimisation support.

Longitudinal Performance

0–60, overtaking, gradeability and top speed measurement with full instrumented data capture.

Ride — Wheel Rates & Dampers

Corner weight, wheel rate, damper bench characterisation and on-vehicle correlation to target ride performance.

Handling

Double lane change, steady-state cornering, step steer and transient handling assessment to ISO standards.

Steering & Turning Circle

Static and dynamic steering effort, lock-to-lock characterisation and minimum turning circle assessment.

Brake Performance

Stopping distance, brake fade assessment and regenerative braking performance and feel evaluation.

Road Load Data Acquisition

Full RLDA campaigns on customer or representative routes with QA and delivery in customer-specified formats.

RLDA to Accelerated Test

Rainflow counting, damage equivalence and rig programme development from RLDA data sets.

Accelerated Durability

Accelerated durability route development and management, alongside rig-based durability programme support.

HET / CET

Hot and cold environment testing programme management and execution for thermal and HVAC systems.

Ergonomics & Occupant Comfort

Reach and vision analysis, WBV measurement and occupant comfort subjective evaluation.

Wind Tunnel Testing

Aerodynamic drag, lift and cross-wind sensitivity with correlation to road-based coast-down testing.

HMI & UI Assessment

Driver HMI usability, display legibility, control intuitiveness and driver workload assessment.

NVH

In-cabin and exterior NVH measurement, source-path-receiver analysis and pass-by noise assessment.
