Who we are

Built the vehicle.
Bought the assets.
Still here.

EVT Tech Ltd is formed by the former Volta Trucks UK engineering leads — the team responsible for designing and validating the Volta Zero from 2020 through to production. When Volta Trucks ceased trading in 2023, we acquired the UK vehicle fleet, battery stock and workshop equipment and continued independently.

We operate from Horiba MIRA in Nuneaton — one of Europe’s most comprehensive automotive test facilities — with full workshop capability, owned instrumentation and the deepest possible knowledge of the Volta Zero platform.

Volta Trucks ceased trading in 2023. EVT Tech acquired the UK assets directly and operates as an independent company. Our engineers built and validated the vehicle — that knowledge remains with us.
